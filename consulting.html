About
The goal of this project was to design and develop a single-page website for a business advisor specializing in consultancy services for large companies and providing guidance on business plans, financial models, and business processes. The website has a smooth, user-friendly design and clear layout and is accessible on both mobile and desktop devices.
Technologies
HTML5, SASS, Grid, Flex, Bootstrap, Figma
Project Team
7 front-end developers, 1 team lead
Responsibilities
  • Communicated with a client to identify the requirements and transferred them to developers and team lead
  • Designed and coordinated with a client a template in Figma
  •  Constructed a segment of a single-page website, integrating a responsive layout via the utilization of Grid
  • Ensured that the website is 100% cross-browser compatible
